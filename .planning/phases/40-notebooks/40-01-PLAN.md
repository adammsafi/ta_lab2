6. `load_regimes(engine, asset_id: int, tf: str, start: str, end: str) -> pd.DataFrame` -- Queries `cmc_regimes WHERE id=:id AND tf=:tf AND ts >= :start AND ts <= :end ORDER BY ts`. Returns ts-indexed DataFrame with l2_label and any other columns.

**Cell 7 (markdown): Section -- What Are Adaptive Moving Averages?**
- Explain that traditional EMAs use fixed smoothing. AMAs adapt their smoothing speed based on market conditions.
- Brief description of each type:
  - KAMA: Uses Efficiency Ratio to speed up in trends, slow down in noise
  - DEMA: Double exponential -- reduces lag via 2*EMA(n) - EMA(EMA(n))
  - TEMA: Triple exponential -- even less lag
  - HMA: Hull Moving Average -- uses weighted moving averages of different periods to reduce lag while maintaining smoothness

**Cell 8 (code): Compute all 4 AMA types**
