### 4.1 MaxDD Gate Failure: Root Cause Analysis

**What Failed:** Both selected strategies (ema_trend 17/77 and 21/50) fail the V1 MaxDD gate of <= 15%. Worst-fold drawdowns reach 70-75%.

**Why:** EMA crossover strategies are trend-following. During extended bear markets (2018, 2022), the strategy enters long positions on false crossover signals, each resulting in losses. The structural problem is that crypto bear markets last 12-18 months -- longer than any reasonable stop-loss can accommodate without being triggered repeatedly.
